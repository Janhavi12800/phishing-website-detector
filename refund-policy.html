Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective date: 12 June 2026

7-day money-back guarantee

Not happy with Pro? Tell us within 7 days of your purchase and we will refund the full amount — no questions asked.

How to request a refund

  1. Contact us via GitHub within 7 days of payment.
  2. Include the email address you used to log in and the approximate date of payment (a payment reference/receipt helps but is not required).
  3. We confirm the purchase and process the refund to your original payment method.

Timelines

  • Refund requests are reviewed within 2 business days.
  • Once processed, the money typically reaches your account in 5–7 business days (depends on your bank or card provider).
  • Your Pro access is removed when the refund is processed; the free features keep working normally.

Notes

  • The 3-day free trial exists so you can test every Pro feature before paying — we encourage you to use it first.
  • After the 7-day window, refunds are given only where required by law.
  • This is a one-time purchase, not a subscription — there is nothing recurring to cancel.
